Aadhaar Enabled Payment Services (AEPS)

Lets Pay offers the opportunity for any AEPS agent to transform their store into an Aadhaar ATM. By using their Aadhaar card and thumb authentication, customers can securely withdraw cash, obtain a mini statement, check their account balance, and even make payments. As an AEPS agent with Lets Pay, you can earn a substantial commission for each transaction performed through the Aadhaar ATM.

Convert your Store into ATM

Lets Pay's Mini ATM machine provides an excellent solution for merchants who desire to receive card payments anytime, anywhere, while also providing additional services to their customers. By using the Micro ATM machine, you can transform your store into an ATM and offer cash withdrawal services directly to your customers.

Domestic Money Transfer (DMT)

Lets Pay offers a fantastic opportunity for entrepreneurs to start a money transfer business. As a Lets Pay agent, you can transfer cash from your outlet to any bank account across the country, with the lowest charges in the industry, resulting in a high-income opportunity. With each transaction, you, as a retailer, can earn a substantial income.

UPI, QR Code, MPOS, Aadhar Pay

Lets Pay partners can provide their customers with various payment options for their convenience, including a unique UPI & QR Code that customers can scan to make payments. In addition, MPOS is available for debit/credit card swipes, payment links for online payments, and Aadhaar Pay, which enables customers to pay using their Aadhaar card, among many other payment options.

Recharge, Bill Payments, PAN Card

Lets Pay provides its partners with a wide range of services, including customer mobile and DTH recharge, electricity and gas bill payments, as well as PAN card creation services. By offering these services, partners can attract more customers and expand their service portfolio while earning money on each transaction.

Risk Free Guaranteed Returns

Lets Pay offers a risk-free, guaranteed return insurance savings plan with a 25 times cover, providing greater returns than any standard savings account. As a Lets Pay partner, you can increase your income and help customers achieve their savings goals with this plan.

Debit Card without Account

Lets Pay partners can provide customers without a bank account with a debit card by using the Lets Pay Prepaid card. Customers can use this card to make purchases just like a standard debit card.
